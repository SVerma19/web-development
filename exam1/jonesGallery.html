Gallery 2: Jones

This page will tell all about some prominent members of the Jones family.

Jackson Jones

About Jackson

Jackson Jones is the one of the eldest of the Smith family to live in America for a majority of their lives. Jackson was born early in the 20th centruy and married Sophia Jones shortly after his arrival in America. He worked all of his life supporting his family and forging a better future his children.

Sophia Jones

About Sophia

Sophia Jones, similar to her husband, came to America at a young age to work for a better life for her and her children. Shortly after Sophia married Jackson, she gave birth two three children, two boys and one girl, and raised them alongside working multiple part-time jobs.
