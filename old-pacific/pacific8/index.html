Enjoy Nature in Luxury

Pacific Trails Resort offers a special lodging experience on the California North Coast with panoramic views of the Pacific Ocean. Your stayat Pacific Trails Resort includes a sumptuously appointed private yurt and a cooked-to-order breakfast each morning.

  • Explore the coast on your own or join our guided tours
  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying complimentary appetizers
  • Savor nightly fine dining with an ocean view
