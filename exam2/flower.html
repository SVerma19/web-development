The vines mostly hanging up and reaching the top are ideal to be grown on the patio as well as the small garden. Clematis has variations that include Henry, Jackmanii, and General Sikorski. Apart from this, the woody climbing lianas can thrive well in the cool, damp surroundings under the full sun. They are mostly found in the Northern part of the world relatively temperate. Vines produce flowers in mid June and often again in late summer. Some late-flowering clematis vines start to bloom in mid-June and continue through autumn.
